Y-House Concept

Client

- (concept)

Architects

Adamson Associates Architects, ArchitEco + Partners

Location

Taiwan

Y House looks set to become the focal point of a new residential development, serving as a weekend retreat for city workers. Situated in Northeast Taiwan, the 330m2 villa gives an identity to an otherwise nondescript site, standing out amongst the rest and marking the development from a distance. A 40-minute drive from Tainan city and the sea, the Y house is a single-family residence for those wishing to spend their weekends in this futuristic space, on the top of Y-shaped floors and who enjoy cooking in the open kitchen. A special attraction is the swimming pool on the roof cradled in the hollow of the Y.
